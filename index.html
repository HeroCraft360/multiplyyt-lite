About MultiplyYt

MultiplyYt is a browser-based tool that allows users to watch and control multiple YouTube videos simultaneously in a single dashboard. Users can add YouTube links, synchronize playback, save layouts, and manage multiple videos for studying, comparison, or entertainment.

MultiplyYt is free to use. Optional tips help support continued development and future improvements to the platform.
